Music Performance

Johannes've played music instruments for more than 15 years. The China Conservatory of Music granted Johannes certificates in Violin and Cello Performance when he was in high school. JohanensLiu also served as a viola player in the University Symphony Orchestra working with sophisticated musicians at HUST, and he performed in notable events such as the HUST 2019 New Year Concert. He earned first place (national level) with the Symphony Orchestra in the 6th national college student art exhibition. He's especially fond of Bach's.
